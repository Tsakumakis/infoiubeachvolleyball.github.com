About us

We are a small university team that is trying to "revive" volleyball in Corfu. By doing this tournament this enhances the volleyball comunity of our island and hopefully make new players fall in love with the sport.

Registration Info

To register for the tournament, you must be a student or a friend of the Ionian University. Please complete the registration form available on our website from May 6th to May 16th (ATTENTION: No applications will be accepted after May 16th). You will need to provide your team name, player names, and contact information. Once you submit the form, you will be notified of your match schedule within 2 weeks. It is also important to let us know if you have played for an indoor volleyball team or participated in a tournament in the past.

Advice

Our Co-ed Division is all about having a great time and meeting new people. We’re specifically looking for more guys to join the mix—so if you’ve been hesitant because you’re not a 'pro' yet, this is the spot for you.

Important

We strongly advice you to come 30 minutes earlier that your sceduled match so we can be sure that you are there and can warmup properly

Rules

Here are the rules for the tournament:

  • Teams must consist of two players. (No substitute)
  • Matches will be played in a best-of-three sets format up to 21 points.
  • Any disputes will be resolved by the tournament officials.
  • For the Men's and Women's categories, the rules of official EOPE handbook will apply

(Rules)

Co'ed Rules:

  • Teams must consist of four players (two men two women)
  • Matches will be played in a best-of-three sets format up to 21 points.
  • No limitation play, play however you want
  • The same rules as Men's and Women's categories will be applied

Place and time

The tournament will be held in N.A.O.K during the 23rd-24th of May (May 23rd for Co-ed and May 24th for Men\'s and Women\'s categories). The time of the matches will be from 9am to 5pm.
